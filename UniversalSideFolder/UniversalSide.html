This is another particular favorite in the reporting room, espcily for one of our reporters. "The Rockit", as we call it, is an outdoor coaster with a a steep drop, twists, turns, and amazing music! While music tastes do vary, in the reporting room, we find that the country catagiory syncs up with the ride the most. The other categories are club, pop, rap, and of course classic rock.
