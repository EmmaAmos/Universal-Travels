Universal Travels

The Unofficial Travel Guide for the Universal Orlando Parks

What is on the Islands' Side?

The Incredible Hulk Coaster

A thrilling roller coaster with multiple inversions and high speeds.

Doctor Doom's Fearfall

A drop tower ride that launches you up and drops you down rapidly.

Storm Force Accelatron

A spinning ride similar to the classic teacup rides.

The Amazing Adventures of Spider-Man

A 3D dark ride that combines motion simulation and special effects.

Jurassic Park River Adventure

A water ride that takes you through the world of Jurassic Park.

Popeye & Bluto's Bilge-Rat Barges

A white-water rafting ride that will get you soaked.

Dudley Do-Right's Ripsaw Falls

A log flume ride with a big drop at the end.

Hagrid's Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure

A roller coaster that takes you through the Forbidden Forest.

Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey

A dark ride inside Hogwarts Castle with impressive animatronics and effects.

Flight of the Hippogriff

A family-friendly roller coaster in the Wizarding World of Harry Potter.

The High in the Sky Seuss Trolley Train Ride!

A gentle ride through Seuss Landing with great views of the park.

Caro-Seuss-el

A whimsical carousel featuring characters from Dr. Seuss books.
